Our Acrylic MetalPrints uses 4 Stainless Steel posts, one for each corner. The screws go through the posts and into the wall to hang.

We offer 3 surfaces, each with their own unique characteristics.

High Gloss

Brilliant white high gloss surface for unsurpassed detail and vibrance. Our most popular surface.

Mid-Gloss

Similar color intensity and detail as our High Gloss option, with reduced glare.

Satin

Smooth, very even and soft appearance with minimal glare.
